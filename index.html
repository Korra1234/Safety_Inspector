Project Overview

Someone else
is still in
the Facility.

Safety Inspector is an atmospheric first-person horror experience set inside a dark research facility. Built around exploration, environmental storytelling, and radio-based narrative tension rather than direct monster spectacle or constant jump scares.

The player navigates dimly lit medical and research corridors with only a flashlight, moves through gated spaces, searches for clues, and follows a narrative thread through radio dialogue and subtitles. Somewhere inside the same facility, another survivor is trying to reach them.

Isolation, oppressive clinical-industrial space, and the uncertainty of who or what you can trust over the radio form the emotional core of the experience.

Gameplay Architecture

The Core Loop

Every space in the facility is designed around a four-beat loop that sustains tension without relying on constant spectacle. Pressure is environmental, not just reactive.

01

Explore

Navigate dimly lit corridors with only a flashlight. Learn the facility's layout, move through gated spaces, and locate what you need to press forward.

02

Discover

Environmental clues, documents, and physical details tell the story of what happened here. Read the space. Piece it together. The facility itself is the narrator.

03

Communicate

Maintain radio contact with another survivor trapped elsewhere. Branching dialogue creates uncertainty, suspense, and narrative choices that shape how the story unfolds.

04

Survive

Escalating encounter design ensures the facility never feels safe. Tension builds deliberately through pacing and atmosphere rather than relentless spectacle.

Technical Stack

Built for atmosphere
at every level.

Safety Inspector is built on Unreal Engine 5 using a full Blueprint workflow, enabling solo development and rapid iteration without sacrificing visual fidelity or technical ambition.

ENGINE

Unreal Engine 5.6

Full Blueprint architecture enabling rapid solo iteration. UE5's toolset provides production-grade rendering and systems without requiring a large team behind it.

LIGHTING

Lumen Global Illumination

Lumen is central to the atmosphere of Safety Inspector. Dynamic real-time lighting shapes darkness, visibility, dread, and environmental focus throughout every corridor.

GEOMETRY

Nanite Virtualised Geometry

Nanite enables high-fidelity environmental detail at scale, supporting the dense, tactile industrial-clinical worldbuilding the facility demands without performance compromise.

NARRATIVE

Branching Dialogue System

A custom Blueprint-driven dialogue framework manages radio-based narrative states, branching conversation paths, and the evolving relationship between the player and the other voice.

WORKFLOW

Small Team Pipeline

Blueprint-first architecture, version control, and AI-assisted technical direction enable a small team to maintain production quality across systems, environment, and design in parallel.

AUDIO / UI

Subtitle-Driven Presentation

Radio dialogue is delivered through voice and synced subtitles, keeping the player grounded in the space while the narrative unfolds. UI is intentionally minimal and diegetic in tone.
